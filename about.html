Our Story

A Journey Rooted in Faith & Knowledge

Light Upon Light Quran Academy was founded with one noble vision: to make authentic Islamic education accessible to every Muslim, regardless of location. Our platform brings together qualified, experienced teachers and eager learners in a warm, structured, and spiritually enriching environment.

We employ child-friendly teaching methodologies grounded in compassion and Islamic psychology, ensuring that every student — from toddler to adult — progresses at their own pace with personalized attention and care.

Our academy is built on three pillars: authentic knowledge, sincere teaching, and spiritual connection. Every class is an opportunity to draw closer to Allah through His blessed Book.
